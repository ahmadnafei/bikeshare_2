*Make sure you download pandas package and the relevant data files: 'chicago.csv', 'new_york_city.csv' and 'washington.csv'. They are available through this Google Drive link: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1uFxL0SCn_sNgePeSI2oEW7nPiMbIVuyP?usp=sharing

*This script prompts you to enter the city you want to show bikeshare statistics for and asks if you want to 
filter the data by day or month. 
*Descriptive statistics for bikeshare data are shown after entering the required input.
